The OX 1234 Clash of Clans Clan

Track the meteoric rise of OX 1234

Snippet by Bootstrapious

Tsar Young Money

A fearless warrior, Tsar Young Money was the founder of the noble clan. Never one to shy away from battle, he is known for his aggressive use of archers and baloons, as well as his bold declarations of war.
Chancellor Palpatine

Palpatine currently sits as the 2nd most powerful entity in the clan. Known for his painstaking attention to detail and rigorous film study, Palpatines wisdom and love for the game provide strong inspiration for OX 1234.
General Kenobi

Kenobi, currently the number one ranked warrior in the clan. Despite his immense advantage over his fellow warriors, Kenobi is always quick to donate even his most powerful troops. His complete selflessness has kept the clan together, even through the darkest of fortnites. Kenobi is often at odds with Palpatine, warring for supremacy.
Flip, Chobani Flip

Flip is the most feared builder in all the land. As sure as the sun rises, Flip will land the clan 100 builder trophies a day. Flip's powers grow by the day, and he will soon have the ability to destroy his foes at a scale hitherto undreamt of.

Tredyfynn: The Vanquisher

While still a noob, the vanquisher takes great pride in building his defenses. His accumulation of wealth and bond with his kin are greatly appreciated by his brethren, and feared by his foes.
Knight Aramburu

Knight Aramburu is a mythic creature who's suprise ascent to the higher ranks of the clan have elevated OX's profile to the elite status. Famous for always utilizing the best of his clan's reinforcements, Knight Aramburu is never afraid to attack and increase his trophy count.
Duce Tortellini

A being of immense power, Duce Tortellini rose through the ranks of the clan in the blink of an eye. Famous for his careful scouting and ruthless attacks, Tortellini has amounted a fortune that would turn the heads of even a king. OX 1234 thanks their stars every night that Tortellini is on their side.
Sikko Modum

Sikko Modum returns to the clan after a brief hiatus, and his kin are glad to have him back. An experienced warrior, Modum has travelled all the lands, performing reconaissance for OX-1234, risking everything for the good of the clan. While a wildcard by nature, Modum is back for the long haul, ready to work hand in hand with Palpatine to achieve clan greatness.

Obligatory Name Change

Obligatory name change exemplifies all that is right with OX 1234. Consistently one of the most reliable members in the clan wars, change does not focus on menial details such as landscaping, rather directing his attention to major issues such as ensuring the clan's incredible 4-1 war record.
Marethyu

Marethyu is the newest addition to the clan. A wise, mystic creature who resides in the shire, Marethyu excels in hand to hand combat, one of the few weaknesses of OX-1234. While Marethyu has big shoes to fill with the departure of Wombat Wagner, the clan has tremendous faith in his abilities.
Warlord Babatunde

Babatunde is one of the newest additions to the clan. While his experience is lacking, his dedication to the clan is felt in many ways. Never one to shy from a challenge, the future looks bright for Babatunde, given both his most recent plunders and his resilience after being blocked from joining OX-1234.
